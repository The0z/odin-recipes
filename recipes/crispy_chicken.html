Crispy Chicken

Description

Get ready for the ultimate crispy chicken sandwich. Imagine an ultra crispy, tender and juicy, and bursting flavour waking up your taste buds. Frying up the chicken may seem like a chore, but it takes this meal to the next level. This recipe makes 4 burgers.

Difficulty level: 4 out of 5 Chefs

Ingredients

The Burger

  • Four Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Cup Coleslaw
  • 2-3 Bacons Strips per Burger
  • Honey and Sriracha
  • 3 Cups of Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Burger Buns

Wet Mix

  • 1 1/4 cups Buttermilk
  • 1 tsp each for Paprika and Salt
  • 1/2 tsp each for Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, White Pepper, and Cayenne Pepper

Dry Mix

  • 1 3/4 cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp each of Baking Powder, Oregano, Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Pound chicken to 3/4" thickness
  2. Combine all wet mix ingredients and mix together
  3. Fully coat chicken with wet mix and cover with cling wrap. Let sit for 4 hours
  4. Take coated chicken out of fridge 30 minutes before cooking it
  5. Combine and mix all dry ingredients together. Cover coated chicken with the dry mix
  6. In a large pot heat oil to 356F. Carefully place chicken in the hot oil, ensure temp stays at 356F. After 4-5 minutes flip chicken over and continue frying until golden brown
  7. Confirm chicken has reached 165F internally. If not, continue frying
  8. Place crispy chicken on wire rack with paper towel below
  9. Stack burgers as follows: Bottom Bun, Coleslaw, Chicken, Bacon, Honey and sriracha, Top Bun
