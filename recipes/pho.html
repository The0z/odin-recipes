Delicious Pho

Description

Experience the magic of one of the greatest noodle soups in the world with this easy to follow traditional Vietnamese Pho recipe!

Made from scratch with the signature broth that's light yet at the same time so full of flavour, it's infused with spices like cinnamon, star anise and cardamom. The soup is utterly addictive and every spoonful leaves you wanting more!

See recipetineats website for the original recipe.

Difficulty level: 3 out of 5 Chefs

Ingredients

  • Brisket and Meaty Bones
  • Cardamom
  • Coriander
  • Star Anise
  • Ginger
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Fish Sauce
  • Onion
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil the brisket and bones for 5 minutes
  2. Extract the scum off the top of the boiling water
  3. Remove any scum off the bones
  4. Add bones, brisket, water, onion, ginger and spices. Simmer for 3 hours
  5. Remove the brisket
  6. Simmer 40 minutes with just bones
  7. Strain then ladle into bowls over noodles and pile on the toppings
