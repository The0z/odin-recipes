Canadian Pizza

Description

The greatest of all pizza's, the Canadian Pizza. This pizza consists of the meatiest bacon, sausage, and pepperoni combined with mozzarella cheese and mushrooms.

This pizza will make your guest come back for seconds at any type of gathering!

Difficulty level: 2 out of 5 Chefs

Ingredients

  • 250 g of Bacon
  • 1 Sausage Link
  • 500 g of Pepperoni
  • 2 cups of Mozzarella
  • 1 cup of Mushroom
  • Pizza Crust
  • 1 Jar of Pizza Sauce

Steps

  1. Prep the pizza crust according pizza crust instructions
  2. Top each pizza with pizza sauce and mozzarella
  3. Shred bacon into tiny bits and thinly slice the sausage
  4. Add pepperoni, shredded bacon, sliced sausages, and mushrooms to pizza
  5. Bake at 375 F for 25 minutes. Let rest for 5 minutes and enjoy.
